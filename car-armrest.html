Nowadays, car armrests are a popular car accessory in the world. At first, it was used to provide elbow support for passengers. But now, the auto armrest is developed into a storage box in the car, including an armrest panel, which can facilitate conversations, work, and read documents. 

Two Types of Automobile Armrests at Carfugroup

Usually, the auto central armrest has two kinds, front armrest and rear armrest.
The front armrest is set in the middle of the front seat, and the armrest on the front aisle provides elbow support for the front-row drivers and passengers. In-built with drink holders and portable receptacles, these can simply be mounted on the car's gear console. Included with box, these are supplied with a huge storage space so as to make the storage of mobile, money change etc. Providing extra space for ashtray as well as glass holder, these armrests are made of good quality material & have dandified designs.
The rear armrest is located in the middle of the rear seat center armrest, mostly retractable, which can provide elbow space for rear passengers. If you don't need a handrail, you can put it away without affecting the sense of space in the car. Some models are equipped with a cup holder or storage box on the rear seat center armrest, and some luxury cars also integrate a human-computer interaction system on the rear seat center armrest to operate the rear audio-visual system.
