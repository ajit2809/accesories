Car mudguard cover is a plate structure installed behind the outer frame of the wheel. the mud guard is usually made of high-quality rubber or engineering plastics.

Types of car mudguard cover

1. Rubber mudguard

Also known as mudguard rubber sheet, rubber sheet used to block mud and sand splashing for vehicles. It is generally pure rubber products, also can be made of rubber, plastic and rubber. Rubber mudguard has better resistance to aging performance, and often used in the rear of various vehicles.

2. Plastic mudguard

As the name suggests, mudguards made of plastic are cheap and hard and fragile.
Painted & painying fender

Advantage of car mudguard cover

Automotive mudguard cover can effectively protect the car body surface from scratches and reduce the splash of mud and water in rainy days.
